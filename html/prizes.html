Prizes

World Artography contest, where photographers around the globe not only become a part of history and enter the Guinness World Book of Records but EACH successful entrant will feature at least one of their images in leading global magazines, newspapers and feature an image in galleries across the globe.

Competition Prizes:

  1. In each Category 3 prizes (1st/2nd/3rd)
    1st - INR 300,000
    2nd – INR 200,000
    3rd - INR 100,000
  2. Jury Choice INR 50,000 (One Each Category)
  3. 2 Special Mentions per category – INR 10,000 each
  4. Public Vote INR 50,000 Best Photo
  5. Top 100 photographers will also be selected to showcase their portfolio at the Qatar 2020 Exhibition (read details below).
  6. Each company will pick 12 images they like (from the remaining non winners) which will be used for 2020/2021 calendars which will be distributed to their clients/business associates/friends & family in time for Christmas 2019.
  7. Once the Grid is full with 5000 images, all the 5000 images will be displayed in a permanent art gallery set up in Qatar, which will be open for display for the exclusive visitors of this art gallery. Every image will also get GBP 100 in return per image submitted on website (read details below).
  8. All entrants’ images from the grid will feature in a one-off 2020 World Artography "Guinness World Book of Record Winners" Best Photographers Of The Year, published by Sony, affiliated with 17 Industry expert and distributed in and across, UK, Europe, America & Asia.
  9. All the images from the website will feature in magazines/newspapers around London Europe, Asia, and America throughout 2020

Magazine Affiliation To Use Images: BBC WILDLIFE, WILD PLANET, WWM, OUTDOOR PHOTOGRAPHY MAGAZINE, BIRDS & BLOOM, ADVENTURE & WILDLIFE, NAT GEO & SMITHSONIA MAGAZINE

  1. International media coverage for winners across all categories.
  2. All entries will be accredited with a Framed Sony World Artography Certificate, with all the affiliated logos as featured footer & website.

Exhibition:
Top 100 photographers will be selected to showcase their portfolio at the Qatar 2020 Exhibition inclusive of 1 Night Bed & Breakfast (based on 4 sharing a room) (Flights/Airport Transfers Not Included) and Exhibition Stand per winner, where they can showcase their selected images other than the one submitted in the contest, which can also be bought by the visitors. This will be a unique opportunity for the selected participants to showcase their work on global platform.

Top 20 in each category will also be selected to showcase their portfolio at the Qatar 2020 Exhibition inclusive of 1 Night Bed & Breakfast (based on 4 sharing a room) and Exhibition Stand per winner (Flights/Airport Transfers Not Included).

Bumper Bonanza:
As mentioned earlier, the Grid of pixels set on the website will accommodate maximum 5000 images. And on successful completion of this entire grid, which means on sale of all 5000 slots on the grid, World Artography will open bumper bonanza for the contestants.

All the 5000 images will be displayed in a permanent art gallery set up in Qatar, which will be open for display for the exclusive visitors of this art gallery. The exclusive rights of these images will be with the owners of this Art gallery with due credit to the image makers. Image maker will hand sign these images in front and the back to ensure its originality and authenticity. Participant will be given £ 100 per image submitted on website by the owners of the gallery, this means that participant is entitled to receive entire amount spent on submission if all the grid is full with 5000 images. Though participant will be the sole owner of this image, he/she can not hand sign and give this image again to any one any time in the world, thus ensuring that the image displayed in the gallery is single signed image in the world.
